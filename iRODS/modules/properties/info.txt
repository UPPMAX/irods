Name:		properties
Brief:		Microservices for managing property lists
Description:	Microservices for managing property lists containing keyword-value pairs.  Property lists are used to hold file attributes and options for controlling other complex microservices.  Property lists may be converted to XML-like strings, or parsed from those strings.
